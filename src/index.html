Here's a list of (recommended) gear to have for your backpacking trip:

Backpack

it's not a backpacking trip without a backpack! You will need a sturdy one since you'll be carrying all your belongings in it for lengthy amounts of time

Hiking Shoes or Boots

Since most of your trip is going to be spent walking and hiking, reliable and comfortable shoes are essential to your adventure. We recomment getting both shoes and boots so you are able to adapt to weather conditions wherever you are.

Tent & Sleeping Bag

You'll need those for the countless camping stops you're probably going to make!

First Aid Kit

You're going to be travelling long distances on foot while carrying your belongings & camping occasionally. It's a good idea to have a first aid kit on you at all times

Travel Stove and Cutlery

For all the time you might be spending in the woods, you will need a travel stove top and assorted cutlery so you can cook your food. Food planning is in the "Food" section of this webpage. Make sure not to get a stove top that's too heavy since you'll be carrying it with you everywhere.

If you need help getting started on your shopping, REI is always a good start. We also reccomend BackCountry Gear.
